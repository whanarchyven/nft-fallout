About
In After the War, you can play as one of five different types of characters, each with their own features, strengths, and weaknesses. Players can fight both solo and in group confrontations. Clan confrontations play a special role in the game.

Clan
system
The game implements a system of clan confrontations in the tournaments to seize territories and passing the quest chains of the main legend of the game. Creation of a clan is a process that requires investment of game currency from each member of the association of players and minimum level 3 of all participants.
The minimum number of participants to register a clan - 8 people. Creators are also automatically included in the Clan Senate, which must choose the head of the association, subsequently guiding many in-game processes.

Discovery mode
After the War is a game capable of scaling. At each stage there will be available global quests, completing which the player or clan can get legendary rewards in the project tokens and unique items.
As the completion of each global quest leads to the opening of new game zones and opportunities.

Gameplay
In PvP mode, you will fight with other players in real time in single and group battles.
In PvE mode will be available to scout and search for enemies and resources on the map. PvE mode is available from level 2 if you have a license "hunter of the wasteland"

equipment
Equipment in the project is represented by 7 types of items, each of which can be replaced depending on the availability of the required parameters and availability in the inventory.
These are visor, helmet, armor plate, pants, boots, gloves, droneб weapon. Equipment can be upgraded through the use of nanobusts, available for purchase in the store and found in the wastelands. Improvement of equipment is available from level 3. The number of boosts in the store is updated daily and can not exceed 20 sets.
